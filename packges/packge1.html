The essence of culture: all-inclusive Sri Lanka 4 days tour

  • Encounter ancient historical monuments, temples, and laid-back villages on this Sri Lanka 4-day adventure through the Sri Lanka cultural triangle.


    Cover the entire circuit of Sri Lanka's cultural triangle, five UNESCO world heritage sites, hundreds of historical marvels, and five cultural cities within four days. Visit the Anuradhapura ancient city, the Golden Temple of Dambulla, the ancient city of Polonnaruwa, the Aukana Buddha statue, Mihintale (the cradle of Buddhism in Sri Lanka), Sigiriya Rock Fortress and see the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa) — all covered within 4 days. Your comprehensive cultural tour itinerary includes a Jeep safari at Minneriya National Park, a spice/herbal garden, Sri Lanka hill country, a Pinnawala elephant orphanage, a jungle trek, tuk-tuk transport, an ox-cart ride in a rural village and many more. Includes either standard, 3-star or 4-star accommodation with daily breakfast and dinner.

Highlights
  • Visit the world's 3rd tallest ancient building (after pyramids) and the tallest Buddhist stupa built in the 2nd century.
  • The proposed 8th-century world wonder, one of the oldest landscaped gardens in the world, dates back to the 5th century.
  • Visit Dambulla, a sacred pilgrimage site for 22 centuries with 157 Buddha statues and paintings (with a surface area of 2100 m2).
  • The Avukana statue (5th century), 14m, is one of the best examples of Buddha statues from ancient Sri Lanka.
  • Visit Mihintale ancient Ayurveda hospital—perhaps the oldest hospital in the world according to Historia Hospitalium 1975, Heinrich E. Müller-Dietz.
  • Attend the evening ceremony of the Tooth relic temple with your guide
  • Kandy walking, sightseeing tour and shopping.
  • Visiting the Kandyan cultural show, witnessing the acrobatic performances of Sri Lankan dancers and listening to the soothing music of musicians.
  • Take a 360-degree panoramic view from the summit of the Sigiriya rock fortress.
  • Walking through the spice/herbal garden while learning about herbal trees, plants and spices
  • Walk through the tea plantations and Royal Botanic Gardens of Peradeniya, Victoria Park and many more.
  • Minneriya Safari Park is in search of wild elephants, crocodiles and other wild creatures with a local guide and driver.
  • Attend the Tooth relic temple evening ceremony with your guide
